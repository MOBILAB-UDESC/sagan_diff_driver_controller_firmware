Motor and Encoder -  Front
RED - Motor Power +
BLK - Motor Power -
GRN - GND encoder
BLU - VCC encoder
YLW - A signal (PIN 14)
WHT - B signal (PIN 15)

Motor driver - 1
RED - VCC
BLK - GND
WHT - Enable (PIN 21)
BRW - Current Sensor (PIN 26)
GRN - Input A (PIN 7)
BLU - Input B (PIN 6)
ORN - PWM (PIN 8)
